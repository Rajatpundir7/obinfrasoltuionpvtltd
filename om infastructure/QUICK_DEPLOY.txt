==========================================
VERCEL DEPLOYMENT - QUICK INSTRUCTIONS
==========================================

STEP 1: LOGIN TO VERCEL
------------------------
1. Open this link in your browser:
   https://vercel.com/login

2. Sign in (or create account if you don't have one)
   - You can use GitHub, GitLab, or Email

3. After logging in, come back here

STEP 2: DEPLOY VIA BROWSER (EASIEST METHOD)
-------------------------------------------
1. Go to: https://vercel.com/new
2. Click "Continue with GitHub" (or your preferred method)
3. Click "Import Git Repository" OR "Browse" to upload
4. If uploading:
   - Drag and drop your entire project folder
   - Click "Deploy"
5. Wait 1-2 minutes
6. Your site will be live!

STEP 3: GET YOUR DEPLOYMENT URL
--------------------------------
After deployment:
- Vercel will show you a URL like: https://ombalaji-infra-website.vercel.app
- This is your live website!

==========================================

ALTERNATIVE: Use Vercel CLI (after login)
==========================================
Run these commands in PowerShell:

1. vercel login
   (Follow browser prompts)

2. vercel
   (Deploy to preview)

3. vercel --prod
   (Deploy to production)
